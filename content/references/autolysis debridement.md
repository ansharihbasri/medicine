
- when moisture balance is kept optimum (see: [[wound dressing]]), enzymes ([[protease]]s and [[matrix metalloproteinase]]s) become more active -> slowly break down dead tissues. if too dry, enzymes not active enough to work; too e.g. due to exudate from infection may wash out enzymes or growth factors, or cause healthy tissue maceration
- works best on soft, non-infected necrotic tissue
