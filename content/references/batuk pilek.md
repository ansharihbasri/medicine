## [[J02.9 acute pharyngitis unspecified]] ([[SKDI 4A]])
- manifestations:
	- pharynx inflammation -> [[sore throat]], etc.
	  gejala khas bdsr etio:
		- [[viral pharyngitis]] (umumnya [[rhinovirus]]):
		  HT: diawali gejala [[coryza (rhinitis)]] lalu bbrp hari kemudian [[pharyngitis]]
		  PE: [[pharynx]] dan [[tonsil]] hiperemis, dapat tanpa eksudat
		- [[bacterial pharyngitis]]:
		  HT: severe [[headache]], [[vomiting]], kadang [[fever]] suhu tinggi, jarang disertai [[cough]] (also see: [[why bacterial pharyngitis no cough compared to viral pharyngitis]])
		  PE: [[fever]] temp, [[tonsil]] membesar, [[pharynx]] dan [[tonsil]] hiperemis disertai eksudat, [[petechiae]] pada [[palatum]] dan [[pharynx]], bisa pembesaran [[cervical lymph nodes]] anterior kenyal dan nyeri tekan
		- [[fungal pharyngitis]]:
		  HT: terutama [[sore throat]] dan nyeri menelan
		  PE: plak putih di [[oropharynx]] dan pangkal lidah, mukosa [[pharynx]] lainnya hiperemis
		- lain-lain (see PPK 2017 p. 239)
- penunjang see p. 240
- diagnosis criteria: PPK 2017 p. 240, bdsr. HT, PE, penunjang (bila perlu) di atas.
- treatment: see [[J02.9 acute pharyngitis unspecified#^c3ba0b]]

## [[J04.0 acute laryngitis]] ([[SKDI 4A]])
- manifestations:
	- [[larynx]] inflammation ->
	  HT: suara serak atau hilang suara ([[aphonia]]), [[dyspnea]], [[stridor]], [[sore throat]] terutama saat bicara atau menelan, [[dry cough]] yang lama2 disertai dahak kental (why ...), gejala [[J00 acute nasopharyngitis (common cold) (acute rhinitis)]]
	  PE: [[indirect laryngoscopy]]: [[larynx]] mucosa hiperemis dan edema, [[nasopharynx]] or [[paranasal sinus]] tanda radang (hiperemis, ...)
	- systemic inflammation ->
	  HT: [[fever]], [[malaise]]
	  PE: [[temperature]]
- penunjang see p. 243
- diagnosis criteria: bdsrkan HT, PE, penunjang (bila perlu) di atas
- treatment: see: [[J04.0 acute laryngitis#^50a136]]

## [[J20.9 acute bronchitis unspecified]] ([[SKDI 4A]])
- manifestations (PPK 2017 p. 248):
	- bronchi inflammation -> mucus hypersecretion ->
	  HT: [[cough]] (dry or mucous) 2-3 weeks, mucus clear/white/yellowish/green-ish, [[bloody cough]] if airway irritation, mild [[fever]], [[chest discomfort]], [[dyspnea]], [[wheezing]] esp. after [[cough]]ing
	  PE:
		- [[temperature]];
		- [[thorax inspection]]: [[barrel chest]] (also see: [[why barrel chest in acute bronchitis]]);
		- [[thorax palpation]]: normal [[tactile fremitus]];
		- [[thorax percussion]]:
			- [[sonor]]
			- reduced [[diaphragmatic excursion (peranjakan hati)]] (also see: [[why reduced diaphragmatic excursion (peranjakan hati) in acute bronchitis]])
			- lowered [[liver-lung border (batas paru hepar)]] (also see: why [[lowered liver-lung border (batas paru hepar) in acute bronchitis]])
- [ ] penunjang (see p. 249) harus ga?
- diagnosis criteria: based on HT, PE, penunjang
- treatment: see [[J20.9 acute bronchitis unspecified#^4bef3c]]

## [[J30.9 allergic rhinitis unspecified]] ([[SKDI 4A]])
- manifestations:
	- allergic inflammation of nasopharyngeal mucosa ->
	  HT: [[trias alergi]] ([[rhinorrhea]], [[sneezing]], hidung tersumbat dan gatal hidung), [[sneezing]] berulang (> 5x) terutama pagi hari, mata gatal atau banyak air mata.
	  RF: riwayat [[allergy (atopy)]], lingkungan lembab dapat tumbuh jamur dan menyebabkan gejala alergi, paparan debu (karpet, seprai, ...)
	  PE:
		  general: tampak menggosok2 hidung karena gatal
		  ... (banyak klo detil so see PPK 2017 p. 284)
