- self-limiting inflammation of [[peripheral nerve]]s i.e. [[myelin sheath]] (or [[axon]]) due to [[cross reactivity]] via [[mimicry protein]] from [[infections]] e.g. [[Campylobacter jejuni]] and stuff. in a sense, is sort of [[type II hypersensitivity]].

# diagnosis
- clinically:
	- hx of infection
	- [[ascending paralysis]]
	- symmetry
	- ...
- lab:
	- [[albuminocytologic disassociation dissatisfaction]] ([[lumbar puncture]] shows CSF high protein but low WBC—contrast to e.g. [[why neonatal meningitis lumbar puncture is high protein, low glucose, WBC positive]])
# compli
- respiratory distress

# tx
- [[plasmapheresis]]
- [[intravenous antibodies]]/[[IVIG]]
