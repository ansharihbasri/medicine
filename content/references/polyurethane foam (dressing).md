
- fungsi
	- semipermeable: gas permeable, microorganism and fluid nope
	- [[waterproof dressing]]
	- can be [[adhesive dressing]]
	- doesn't leave residue -> i.e. won't leave small traces when removed. prevents infection, irritation
	- more absorbent, moderate to very high exudate
	- ok for infected wounds -> absorbs pus etc. keep wound clean and dry
	- prevent [[hypergranulation]] since it keeps heavy exudate wounds balanced moisture-wise. too much exudate, too much fluid -> impede healing, prolonged inflammation -> [[hypertrophic scar]] due to [[hypergranulation]].
