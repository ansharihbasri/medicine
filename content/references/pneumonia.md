# PE

- inspect: tertinggal waktu napas (lagging?)
- palpate: increased [[tactile fremitus]] in painful area (also see: [[why consolidated lung cause increased tactile fremitus]]

- aus: [[bronchovesicular sound]] or even [[bronchial breathing sound]] (see: [[why consolidated lung cause bronchial sounds instead of vesicular]]); may also be [[rhonchi]] (wet, due to consolidation?)
