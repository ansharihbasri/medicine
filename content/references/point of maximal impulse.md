# physical examination
- if impalpable at supine, try [[left lateral decubitus position]]
- variables: (Bates 13th, p. 865/2145)
	- area diameter: <2.5 cm in supine (may be larger in [[left lateral decubitus position]])
		- diffuse [[PMI]] >3 cm may indicate left ventricular enlargement
	- amplitude & duration: normal: a brisk tap. watch if it's hyperkinetic: forceful, ends before systole
