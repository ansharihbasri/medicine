
[[Neer's test]] positive = something in [[subacromial space]] hurts when compressed, but don't know exactly yet, could be: [[supraspinatus]], [[subacromial bursa]], long head of [[biceps]] tendon, possibly even [[acromioclavicular (AC) joint]]. To isolate better [[supraspinatus]], [[Hawkins test]] is done: it pinches [[supraspinatus]] tendon much more directly under [[coracoacromial arch]], while other structures like [[biceps]] tendon are less involved this way.
