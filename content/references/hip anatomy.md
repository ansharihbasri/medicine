
# muscles
- [[hip flexors]]:
	- [[iliopsoas muscle]] (or [[iliacus muscle]] and [[psoas major]])
	- [[rectus femoris]]
	- some of [[sartorius]]
- femur external/lateral rotator:
	- [[sartorius]]
	- (some of?) [[gluteus maximus]]
- femur internal/medial rotator:
	- (some of?) [[gluteus medius]] and [[gluteus minimus]]
- [[hip extensors]]:
	- [[hamstrings]]
	- [[gluteus maximus]]
	- [[gluteus medius]] (said Bates)
	- [[adductor magnus]] (Bates)
- [[femur abductors]]
	- [[gluteus medius]] (said Sam Webster)
	- [[gluteus minimus]]
	- [[tensor fascia latae (TFL)]]
