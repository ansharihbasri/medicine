# history taking & PE
- long term signs of dysfx
	- general
		- fatigue, weakness ([[anemia]], [[uremia]]) -> PE: pallor, conjunctiva
		- poor appetite, [[nausea]] ([[uremia]]) -> weight loss, PE: weight, BMI
		- [[pruritus]] ([[uremic toxin]]) -> PE: skin scratches, dry skin
		- confusion, drowsiness (advanced CKD)
	- fluid related
		- leg swelling, face puffiness -> PE: edema signs, extremity
		- [[dyspnea]], [[orthopnea]] ([[pulmonary edema]]) -> PE: crackles lung base
		- cardiac: PE [[s3 gallop]] (fluid overload)
	- urinary
		- [[nocturia]]
		- decreased [[urine output]] (later stages)
	- bone pain, fractures ([[renal osteodystrophy]])
	- extremity: [[asterixis (flapping tremor)]] if severe [[uremia]]
- RF
	- [[hypertension]] -> PE: [[blood pressure]]
	- [[diabetes mellitus type 2 (DMT2)]] -> PE: [[blood glucose]]
	- [[NSAID]], [[herbal]] use
	- fam hx of kidney dx
	- medication list (to adjust given CKD)

# diagnosis
jd yg aku ingat (zoom dr Ratna, yakes telkom)
- kl pasien hipertensi atau dm lgsg periksa kreatinin sm albumin urin atau ACR utk skrining CKD
- klo kreatinin ACR jelek cek rutin sambil kontrol hipertensi dmnya, klo dlm 3 bulan kreatinin ACR masih jelek brti masuk CKD (tp liat lg kriteria diagnosis CKD di ppt)
- kontrol hipertensi dm betul2, CKDnya kasi sglt2i, ACEi/ARB, MRA klo albuminuria brp gt dll
- nutrisi CKD 4 low protein diet 0.6, CKD 5 very low 0.4 + suplemen asam amino esensial atau ketoanalog
- klo anemia krn CKD (normokrom normositik) injeksi epo
- indikasi rujuk ke KGH liat ppt
- indikasi HD liat ppt klo indikasi mulai edukasi2, pasang akses HD dll
