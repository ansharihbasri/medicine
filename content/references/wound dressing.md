
- concept: moisture balance
- dressing choice purpose:
	- buang jaringan mati, benda asing, partikel
	- kontrol infeksi, lindungi luka dari trauma dan invasi bakteri
	- pertahankan kelembaban
	- percepat healing
	- absorb cairan luka
	- nyaman digunakan, mengurangi nyeri
	- proteksi periwound
	- kontrol bau
- lapisan:
	- balutan luka
	- balutan primer
	- balutan sekunder
- bahan balutan:
	- [[secondary wound dressing]]:
		- [[gauze (kassa)]]
	- non-adherent contact layer/first layer
		- if need to add moisture (rehydrate) ("minus exudate"): [[hydrogel (dressing)]]
		- if no exudate: [[transparent film (dressing)]]
		- if low to moderate exudate: [[hydrocolloid (dressing)]]
		- [[alginate (dressing)]] forms into gel like [[hydrocolloid (dressing)]] but more absorbent than [[hydrocolloid (dressing)]] (so moderate to heavy) -> and since turns into gel, good for when you want to absorb (and lock in) fluid rather than just retain it e.g. ulcers
		- if moderate to heavy exudate: [[polyurethane foam (dressing)]] (doesn't form into gel), more cushioning and protective aspect
			- [[alginate vs foam (dressing)]]
		- if bleeding, need hemostatic: [[calcium alginate (dressing)]]
