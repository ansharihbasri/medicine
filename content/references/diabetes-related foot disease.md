# pathogenesis
- includes one or more of the following:
	- [[peripheral neuropathy]]
	- [[peripheral artery disease (PAD)]]
	- [[infection]]
	- [[ulcer]]
	- [[neuro-osteoarthropathy]]
	- [[gangrene]]
	- [[amputation]]
- limited joint mobility due to process of glycation of [[collagen]] (ACSM p. 385)
