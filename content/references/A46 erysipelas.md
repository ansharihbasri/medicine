# s/sx
- well demarcated red skin. (contrast to [[L03.9 cellulitis]])
# etiopathogenesis
- [[Streptococcus pyogenes]] (almost always [[group A beta-hemolytic streptococci]]) (Paul Bolin)
- of superficial dermis (contrast to deep dermis of [[L03.9 cellulitis]]) ^d07bd8
- skin break entry.
- also spread via lymphatic vessels
