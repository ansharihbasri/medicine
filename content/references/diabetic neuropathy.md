# treatment
- prevention via glycemic control
- tx is less than satisfactory
- exercise, diet has some efficacy in [[distal symmetrical polyneuropathy (DSPN)]] in [[diabetes mellitus type 2 (DMT2)]]
- treat [[hypertension]] and [[hypertriglyceridemia]]
- avoid neurotoxins (alcohol), [[smoking (rokok)]]
- supplementation for possible deficiencies e.g. [[vitamin B12]], [[folic acid]]
- educate px that [[loss of protective sensation (LOPS)]] increase ulceration risk, should be prevented
	- for those w s/sx of neuropathy/LOPS, should:
		- check feet daily
		- precautions (footwear)
		- check to podiatrist if foot deformities present
- for pain a/w neuropathy:
	- [[duloxetine]]
	- [[pregabalin]]
