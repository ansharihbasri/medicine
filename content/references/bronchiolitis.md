# risk factor
- highly contagious via hand-carried secretions (aerosols also work) (Nelson Essen 9th p. 418-9)
	- inquire [[exposure to sick people or contagion or similar complaints or disease in community]]
		- from adults w [[URTI]] or children from day care -> inquire [[living with whom and social contact]]
- age: first 2 years, peak at 2-6 mo (Nelson Essen 9th p. 419) ^2c2efe

# nat hx
Nelson Essen 9th p. 419
- incubation period: 4-6 d
- start w prodrome similar to [[J00 acute nasopharyngitis (common cold) (acute rhinitis)]] ([[cough]], [[rhinorrhea]])
	- or [[apnea]] in infants
- over 3-7 d progress to noisy, raspy breathing w [[wheezing]], [[nasal flaring]], [[low-grade fever]], [[irritability]] ^cc62a2
	- [ ] also [[intercostal retractions]], [[suprasternal retractions]] but why?
	- [[air trapping]] thus [[hyperexpansion of the lung]] and [[hypersonor]] during percussion; diffuse [[wheezing]] and [[crackles]] during aus. ^04a403
	- more severe: [[grunting]] and [[cyanosis]]
