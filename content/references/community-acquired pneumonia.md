
# etiology
- clinically hard to distinguish viral vs bacterial (Cotran Robbins 10th p. 706)
	- most common: [[Streptococcus pneumoniae]] aka [[pneumococcus]]
	- if viral, [[URTI]] viruses can extend to [[LRTI]]; see risk factors below
	- often bacterial follows viral [[URTI]] (see [[mostly or most commonly viral URTI]]) -> inquire [[other medical history]] for recent [[URTI]] or [[URTI symptoms]]

# risk factor
- Cotran Robbins 10th p. 707:
	- [[extremes of age]]
	- [[malnutrition]]
	- [[alcoholism]]
	- underlying debilitating illnesses
