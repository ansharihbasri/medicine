# s/sx
- [[persistent cough]] [(NCBI)](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK448067/)
	- 2-3 w (PPK p. 249)
	- w no signs of [[pneumonia]] or [[COPD]]
	- w or w/o [[sputum]]
		- [[clear sputum]] or [[yellowish sputum]] (PPK p. 249: may also be [[greenish sputum]])
	- due to [[increased mucus production]]
- [[wheezing]] [(NCBI)](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK448067/)
- [[low-grade fever]] [(NCBI)](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK448067/)
- [[myalgia]] [(NCBI)](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK448067/)
- [[chest discomfort]] (PPK p. 249)
- [[bloody cough]] if irritation occurs (PPK p. 249)

# etiology
- often viral (90-95%) [(NCBI)](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK448067/)
	- [[J00 acute nasopharyngitis (common cold) (acute rhinitis)]] agents
	- [[J11 influenza due to unindentified virus]]
	- [[rhinovirus]]
	- etc

# pathogenesis/risk factor
- all age but usually > 5 y/o (PPK p. 248)
- via droplet [(NCBI)](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK448067/)
	- inquire [[exposure to sick people or contagion or similar complaints or disease in community or crowd place]]
- irritant or pollutant might also incite or exa
	- inquire [[irritant or pollutant exposure such as smoke etc]]
- [[GERD]] may incite as well (PPK p. 248)
	- inquire [[GERD history]], [[other medical history]]

# nat hx
- cough may persist for 10-20 d [(NCBI)](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK448067/)
