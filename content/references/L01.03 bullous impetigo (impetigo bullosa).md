[[SNPPDI 4]]


- distribution: lokalisata/regioner
- predilection: lipat kulit (aksila, inguinal, gluteal). also possible: palm or soles
	- compare to:
		- [[B08.1 molluscum contagiosum]] (similarly lipat tubuh, but differ in morphology (papule: soft, umbilicated))
		- [[A63.0 condyloma acuminata]] (genitalia but may be close, but differ in morphology (papule: verruca/cauliflower-like)
- spread: (depends on autoinoculation)
- type: [[vesicle]], [[bulla]] (awal jernih kemudian purulen, flaccid, mengilap, dasar eritem), annular squames (scale, left over skin rim) if [[bulla]] rupture can confluent and become polycyclic
