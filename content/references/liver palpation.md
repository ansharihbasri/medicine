- identify liver edge (Bates 13th p. 1056)
	1. start well below percussed lower border
	2. right hand lateral to [[rectus abdominis]] (make sure to not mistake it from the liver)
	3. patient breath w abdomen (to contract diaphragm and push liver down)
	4. palpate upwards
	5. measure distance of edge from right costal margin at [[MCL]] ^fb3472
		- normally about 3 cm during inspiration
	6. observe contour and texture (normally sharp, soft, flat, regular)
- hooking technique esp for obese patients
