[[SNPPDI 4]]


- distribution: lokalisata/regioner
- predilection: regio tubuh yg terbuka (wajah sekitar hidung-mulut, ekstremitas)
	- similar to: ^2ec718
		- [[B00 herpes simplex]] (lips, also may be similar in morphology (vesicle, crust). but may differ slightly in location (herpes border b/w lips and skin vs impetigo more outwards peri-oral); herpes a/w **pain/tingling sensation/prodrome** while impetigo none; impetigo is more prominent w its honey-colored crust and looks 'messier' compared to herpes crust)
- spread: spread outward/centrifugally due to autoinoculation (garuk2)
- type: [[macule]] (erythematous), [[vesicle]], [[pustule]], [[crust]] (honey-colored)
	- may be similar to:
		- macule/papule, vesicle, crust of:
			- [[B01 varicella (cacar air)]] (differ in distribution (generalisata))
			- [[B02 herpes zoster (cacar air)]] (differ in predilection (unilateral, dermatomal))
		- papule, vesicle, crust of: [[B00 herpes simplex]] (but differ in predilection (genitalia) (if lips, see: [[L01.01 non-bullous impetigo (impetigo krustosa)#^2ec718]]))
		- macule/papule, vesicle, ulcer of [[B08.4 hand foot and mouth disease]] (but differ in: HFMD also mouth inside whereas impetigo only outside and perioral; HFMD no honey-colored crust like impetigo...)
