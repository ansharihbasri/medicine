
- distribution: lokalisata or regioner
	- if local, similar to:
		- [[B07 viral warts - verruca vulgaris aka kutil aka common wart]] (but differ in that it predilects [[B07 viral warts - verruca vulgaris aka kutil aka common wart#^8ba5c7|skin/mucosa/nails]])
- spread/predilection: dimana pun tp lebih sering bagian atas batang tubuh: wajah, lipatan (aksila, lipat lutut, selangkangan)
	- similar/compare to:
		- [[L01.03 bullous impetigo (impetigo bullosa)]] (similarly lipat tubuh, but differ in morphology (vesicle, bulla))
		- if selangkangan, may be near [[A63.0 condyloma acuminata]] (genitalia, but differ in morphology (hyperkeratosed/cauliflower-like))
- types: [[papule]] (skin-colored/pink, dome, with central umbilication, can be multiple)
	- compare to:
		- papule of [[B07 viral warts - verruca vulgaris aka kutil aka common wart]] or [[A63.0 condyloma acuminata]] (differ in that verruca is hyperkeratosed/cauliflower-like whereas molluscum is relatively smooth/soft)
		- papule, vesicle, crust of [[B00 herpes simplex#^0ad4c7]] (differ in vesicle and crust, also papule is erythematous whereas molluscum may be skin-colored)
		- maculopapule, vesicle, crust of:
			- [[B01 varicella (cacar air)]] (differ in vesicle and crust, also maculopapule erythematous. also distribution is generalisata.)
			- [[B02 herpes zoster (cacar air)]] (differ in vesicle and crust, maculopapule erythematous. distribution regioner.)
