# etiopathogenesis
- [[measles virus (morbillivirus)]]
- transmission: primarily respiratory droplets over short distances, less commonly suspended small-particle aerosols
	- can be via direct contact w infected secretions
	- doesn't survive long on fomites
- incubation: 10 d to [[fever]] onset; 14 d to rash onset
- infection starts upon viral deposit in [[mucosa]] (resp tract, conjunctiva)

# s/sx
- [[fever]]
- [[cough]]
- [[coryza (rhinitis)]]
- [[conjunctivitis]]
- generalized [[maculopapular]] rash

# diagnosis
## ddx
other causes of [[fever]], rash, [[conjunctivitis]]:
- [[B06 rubella]]:
	- w/o [[cough]]
	- distinctive [[lymphadenopathy]] esp. [[occipital node]] and [[postauricular node]]
- [[scarlet fever]]
- [[Kawasaki disease]]
- [[infectious mononucleosis]]
- [[roseola]]
- [[Rocky Mountain spotted fever]]
- [[enterovirus]] infection
- [[adenovirus]] infection
- [[drug sensitivity]]

# tx
- no specific therapy, only supportive
