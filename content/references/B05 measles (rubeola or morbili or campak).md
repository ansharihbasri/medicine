# etiopathogenesis
- [[measles virus (morbillivirus)]]
- transmission: primarily respiratory droplets over short distances, less commonly suspended small-particle aerosols
	- can be via direct contact w infected secretions
	- doesn't survive long on fomites
- incubation: 10 d to [[fever]] onset; 14 d to rash onset
- infection starts upon viral deposit in [[mucosa]] (resp tract, conjunctiva)

# s/sx
- [[fever]]
- [[cough]]
- [[coryza (rhinitis)]]
- [[conjunctivitis]]
- generalized [[maculopapular]] rash ^5756fb
