
# sx
- [[nephritic syndrome]]

# etiopathogenesis
- following [[strep throat]] or [[skin infection or non-infectious injury]] due to [[Streptococcus pyogenes]] (contrast to [[rheumatic fever]] which can be due to [[strep throat]] but not [[skin infection or non-infectious injury]]) (see: [[why PSGN from both strep throat and impetigo while rheumatic fever only from strep throat]])
- [[type III hypersensitivity]]
