- [ ] bintitan sebelah mana? (pemfis) <- kalo eyelid **edge** cenderung ke [[stye (hordeolum)]] since it's either [[meibomian gland]] or [[Zeis and Moll glands]]; whereas [[chalazion]] is more [[meibomian gland]] so **more deep/down the gland/tarsal plate, inwards**. also [[stye (hordeolum)]]'s infective nature tends to spread inflammation outside (too) whereas [[chalazion]] stays locally deep and inside
- [ ] sejak kapan? <- **akut**: [[stye (hordeolum)]]; **kronis**: [[chalazion]].
- [ ] nyeri/gatal/terasa panas ga? <- **ya:** [[stye (hordeolum)]] (due to inflammation spreading to adjacent areas more richly innervated); **no:** [[chalazion]] (inflammation is contained (granulomatous), and local)
- [ ] sebelumnya pernah seperti ini? apakah sering? <- lebih ke identifying risk factor/susceptibility, also semakin sering, semakin prone to developing [[chalazion]] given the more chance of alterations to the glands due to past inflammations
