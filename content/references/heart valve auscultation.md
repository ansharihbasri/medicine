# technique
- de miftah: use [[stethoscope bell]]
- read in detail: Bates 13th p. 859:
	- [[S1]] = [[tricuspid valve]] & [[mitral valve]] closing = heard louder than [[S2]] at apex (see image below). vice versa: [[S2]] at base.
	- inching stethoscope: start from a point (e.g. base) and establish [[S1]] and [[S2]]. keep the rhythm in mind then inch down the left sternal border and listen for change in sounds (e.g., [[murmur]]).
